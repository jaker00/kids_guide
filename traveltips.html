Travel Tips

  1. Always bring a backpack
  2. Don't forget shoes, sandals, etc.
  3. Bring water! Most travellers get dehydrated very easily.
  4. Bring extra clothes!
  5. Remember where you're staying! Write it down for safekeeping, but somewhere accessible.
